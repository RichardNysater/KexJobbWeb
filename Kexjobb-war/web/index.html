Music comparison

What's the purpose of this page?

We are two computer science undergraduates currently researching Music Information Retrieval.

The purpose is to investigate if we can automatically predict how similar two songs are by using machine learning algorithms.

To train these algorithms we need data from users on how similar some song pairs are, and that is how you can help!

How does it work?

By listening to samples of two songs and then rating how similar you find them, you provide us with the data we need.

As a warmup, you will be presented with three example pairs. After rating a pair you will be shown a suitable rating for that pair.

When you have completed the examples, you will be eligible to rate the main set of song pairs.

In total, there are 10 songs and 45 pairs of songs to rate. You will never be asked to rate the same pair of songs more than once.

Every song pair you rate will help us immensely, so thanks in advance!
